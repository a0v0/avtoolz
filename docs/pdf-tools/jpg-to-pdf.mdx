## About

JPG/JPEG to PDF is a free online tool to convert JPG/JPEG to PDF without losing quality and minimize the size of PDF.

## How to use

1. Select your Images by clicking the "Choose File" button.
2. Select Page Orientation, Page Size, Margin as per your need.
3. Select whether to compress the output pdf.
4. Click "Convert and Download PDF" button to convert and download your PDF.
